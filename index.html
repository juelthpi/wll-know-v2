myfinpadi.com is a property of FinPadi Technologies Limited. We are a technology-driven alternative payments platform where freelancers, merchants and consumers can transact with digital currencies like bitcoin, payoneer, paypal, etc....

To do that you'll need a Myfinpadi.com account, access to Bitcoins in another Bitcoin wallet and you need to know your Myfinpadi.com receiving address.

If all goes well Bitcoin transactions sent with the correct fee take between 10-60 minutes when you are sending Bitcoins to your Myfinpadi wallet. If your transaction is taking longer than this, please check that the fee is correct.

Transactions between Myfinpadi users are instantaneous.

Transactions you send from your Myfinpadi wallet are sent with a high priority fee to ensure they confirm within approximately 30 minutes (within the next three blocks).

Bitcoin transactions are irreversible, once you’ve sent money to another Bitcoin address it is not possible for you or myfinpadi.com to reverse it.
